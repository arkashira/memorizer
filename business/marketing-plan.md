# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
Memorizer: Automated codebase memory usage analysis and optimization for developers.
Alternatives:
1. Memorizer: AI-powered code memory optimization for high-performance applications.
2. Memorizer: Codebase memory usage analysis and optimization for reduced latency and improved user experience.
3. Memorizer: Intelligent code memory management for developers to build faster, more efficient software.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Alex Chen**: Senior Software Engineer at a fintech startup, responsible for optimizing application performance, with a budget of $5,000/month for developer tools.
2. **Maya Patel**: DevOps Engineer at an e-commerce company, focused on reducing latency and improving user experience, with a budget of $3,000/month for optimization tools.
3. **Ethan Kim**: Technical Lead at a gaming studio, prioritizing high-performance application development, with a budget of $10,000/month for premium developer tools.

## Channels
1. **GitHub Ads**: Targeting developers on GitHub with relevant ads, estimated CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) of $50.
2. **Stack Overflow Ads**: Reaching developers through targeted ads on Stack Overflow, estimated CAC of $30.
3. **Developer-focused Podcast Sponsorships**: Partnering with popular podcasts for developers, estimated CAC of $20.

## Content Cadence
Week 1-2: Introduction to Memorizer and its benefits, through blog posts and social media.
Week 3-4: Technical deep dives into Memorizer's features and use cases, through webinars and tutorials.
Week 5-6: Customer success stories and testimonials, through case studies and video interviews.
Week 7-12: Advanced topics, such as customization and integration with other tools, through blog posts and podcasts.

## Launch Milestones
* D-30: Finalize marketing materials, including website, blog, and social media content.
* D-0: Launch Memorizer and announce it on social media, GitHub, and Stack Overflow.
* D+30: Analyze early adoption metrics and adjust marketing strategy accordingly.
* D+90: Evaluate overall performance, including customer acquisition and revenue growth.

## Success Metrics
* DAU (Daily Active Users): 1,000 by D+90.
* $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) target: $100,000 by D+90.
